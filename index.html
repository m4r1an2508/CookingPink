Popular Recipes

Chocolate Chip Cookies

A classic treat loved by all!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 350°F. Mix the ingredients, form dough into balls, and bake for 10-12 minutes.

Spaghetti Bolognese

A hearty pasta dish with rich tomato sauce and minced beef.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 package spaghetti

Instructions:

Cook the spaghetti. In a separate pan, sauté onion and beef, then add tomato sauce. Combine with pasta and serve.
